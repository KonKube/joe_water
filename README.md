# joe_water
GreenIQ with Sprinkler_Pi

Sprinklers Pi Zone  | wPi GPIO  | Cable 1 color     | GreenIQ Valve #
Master/Pump         | `0`       | `Brown`           | `2`
1                   | `1`       | `Red`             | `3`
2                   | `2`       | `Yellow`          | `4`
3                   | `3`       | `Green`           | `5`
4                   | `4`       | `Blue`            | `6`
5                   | `5`       | `Orange`          | `Master`
6                   | `6`       | `White (Cable 2)` |	`Light`
7                   | `7`       | `White (Cable 1)` | `1`
